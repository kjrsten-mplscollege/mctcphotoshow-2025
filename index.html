The Graduating Class

The Photography and Motion (PHMO) program at Minneapolis College is excited to share the work of the Portfolio class of Spring 2025. These portfolios are the culmination of all the skills they have learned throughout their time in this amazing program and are ready to hit their photographic career paths running. Each photographer has developed their own unique creative style and area of focus. Enjoy their work here and to see more click over to their personal websites and leave a message.
