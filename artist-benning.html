Seth Benning

Seth Benning


Seth is a South Dakota-raised photographer who thrives on creating authentic connections through his work. Influenced by his upbringing in the Black Hills, he developed a love for outdoor and landscape photography, which remains his primary focus. Currently studying photography, Seth brings a strong eye for detail and a passion for capturing the natural beauty of the world. He also enjoys exploring his creativity through Photoshop and graphic design, combining these skills to enhance his work.
